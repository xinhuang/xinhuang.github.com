Y Combinator, where the name of the famous VC "Y Combinator" comes from, is a concept from the lambda calculus. It is a very interesting concept proving that recursion is not needed to build recursion.

Like C. P. Snow used knowledge of the Second Law of Thermodynamics as a kind of dividing line between those who were scientifically literate and those who weren't, the Y Combinator can be used as the dividing line in computer science between those who are "functionally literate" and who are not.
